# Resibook

**A living digital record for your home — from inspection report to resale.**

> Portfolio project by [Kevin Lisk](https://github.com/k-lisk), Senior Product Manager. This repository contains a complete product brief for Resibook, a concept I originated to demonstrate marketplace monetization strategy, conversion funnel design, and B2B revenue modeling — skills built across PropTech and advertising marketplace roles.

---

## The 30-Second Version

New homebuyers inherit a house full of unknowns. Sellers can't prove the value of the work they've done. Contractors quote blind. Resibook fixes this by creating a verified, living record of a home — starting the moment a buyer uploads their inspection report — and turns that record into a negotiation asset at resale and a targeted intent signal for home services advertisers.

**The product is free for homeowners.** Revenue comes from a B2B vertical ad marketplace — the first of its kind connecting paint, HVAC, and appliance brands directly to verified, purchase-intent-rich home data.
